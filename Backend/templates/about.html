About Us
Unveiling Brilliance, Inspiring Beauty

Welcome to Silver Stone Jewellery, your premier destination for exquisite silver jewelry pieces. We take pride in offering you a captivating collection of meticulously crafted jewelry that embodies elegance, quality, and timeless style.

At Silver Stone Jewellery, we believe in the power of jewelry to enhance your personal style and make a lasting impression. Each of our silver pieces is handcrafted with utmost care and attention to detail, ensuring that every design is a true masterpiece.

Our team of skilled artisans and designers combines traditional techniques with innovative creativity to bring you a diverse range of jewelry that caters to various tastes and occasions. From delicate silver necklaces and bracelets to stunning rings and earrings, our collection showcases the beauty and versatility of silver.

Hâute Bijoux By Prianca Brand Story

How it all started?

Hâute Bijoux by Prianca pronounced as 'Aut' 'Beejoo' By Prianca is a French name , meaning Trendy Trinkets ... For many years Prianca nurtured a dream : to make a brand which is unique, is different from the rest of the jewelry segment , which defines a whole new statement of wearing jewelry to the woman of Digital world. So her journey began in quest of finding what is the gap in the market. Prianca being a spiritually woven designer, believes in how the universe is intertwined with the aesthetics of design present in our daily life. She learnt all this in an arduous way, a graduate from NIFT ( National Institute Of Fashion Technology, India, prestigious design college), she became a Fashion and Lifestyle Accessories a.k.a , a sort after Jewelry Designer. She then acquired hands on experience by getting an opportunity to work with eminent Brands like Tanishq pvt. Ltd Amrapali Jewels Jaipur, SEEPZ Mumbai. She designed an eclectic , extraordinary theme based-collection of jewellery for Wills Lifestyle Fashion Week ‘2009.
